WHAT IS uRockGuitar?

                uRockGuitar is a company dedicated to provide it's consumers the best quality guitar that can be of use to both beginners and experts. uRockGuitar ensures the quality of the brands that it ships onto the costumer, receiving no complaints for the 10 years it has been of service. From the start of 2009,uRockGuitar has built a steady base of consumers and musicians that are able to acquire an outstanding guitar for a worth-it price.




HISTORY

                 At mid May 2009, uRockGuitar was founded by Agulerra Tecson, a guitar enthusiast and musician. It was said that he experienced the struggle to find a quality guitar for a given price point that was affordable for all. Thus, he founded the uRockGuitar, a company dedicated to provide an outstanding quality guitar for the best price.
